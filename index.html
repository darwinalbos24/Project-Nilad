Nilad Vaccination Center's Goal

Our vaccination center's goal is to provide high quality service the community deserves. We believe that each of us has the right for a quality service regardless of our status in life.

Healthcare is a luxury many Filipinos cannot afford, which is why more often than not, it is always disregarded. This results to a series of complications not just to an individual's health but to the health of the community as well. Nilad Vaccination Center aims to provide the best healthcare services - not just limited to vaccination.

⚜ Mission

"To provide immunization to the community against the Covid-19 virus."

Nilad Vaccination Center aims to vaccinate the community while providing them with factual information. Nilad also aims to take care of the community through the means of healthcare. Aside from walk-ins and registered patients, Nilad also provides drive-thru vaccines for people with vehicle. This is not limited to people with 4-seater cars only, tricycles and motorcycle are more than welcome to come in and avail this service.

This is to eliminate the mindset of elitism surrounding healthcare. We want Nilad Vaccination Center to become a beacon of symbol, health services must not be expensive nor exclusive to a certain class. Public healthcare is meant for the public and that's what our vaccination center stands for.


✤ Vision

"To provide and attend to the healthcare needs of the community"

We believe that this pandemic will eventually come to pass. By that time, Nilad Vaccination Center will not be rendered useless. The goal is to be able to adapt to the situations and needs required in the community. Nilad Vaccination Center holds rooms for changes, we allocated rooms for other services to be offered once the time for Covid-19 is over. Vaccines offered will provide immunization against flue, tubercolosus, mengitis, leprosy, hepatitis, tetanus, polio, pneumonia, etc.

Moreover, we believe that this pandemic is not the last and another one will surely come and Nilad Vaccination Center will be ready to face the challenges and overcome them.
