Floor Plans

This page includes the proposed floor plans for our vaccination center. This shows the spaces allocated inside the lot and how they are placed in order to cater to design problems.


Ground Floormore_vert
Aboutclose

Ground floor is intended for first dose vaccination as well as spaces for drive-thru.


Patient consultation room where patients are checked up regarding their current condition and if they're fit for a vaccine, or must be scheduled for another time.


Orientation room for orienting patients about to get their first dose. Information includes facts and debunking myths about Covid-19 virus.


Observation room for patients that requires assistance before or after getting their first vaccine shot.

Second floor is intended for second dose vaccines and other vaccination services.


Patient consultation room where patients are checked up about their previous vaccine shot. They are also asked if they encountered any complications.


Rooms for other vaccine services are partially offered. Covid-19 vaccination is the main priority.


Observation room for patients that requires assistance before or after getting their second vaccine shot.

Third Floormore_vert

Third floor is intended for staffs and authorized personnel of the vaccine center.


Break room for workers. This allows them to relax and breathe a little.


Meeting room if there is an annoucement to be made or special meetings that must be held.


Isolation room is intended for patients or workers with Covid-19 symptoms but cannot immaediately go to a hospital.

Top floor is reserved for the community court that was originally situated in the lot.


Storage room for all the equipments needed to use the court.
